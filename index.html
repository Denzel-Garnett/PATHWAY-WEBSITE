Exam Tips
Performance-Based Qs
The exam opens with PBQs (drag-and-drop and simulation). Flag them and skip — come back after answering the multiple choice questions first.

Exam Tips
Master Subnetting Cold
You will get subnetting questions on every exam attempt. Practice until you can calculate subnet masks, host ranges, and CIDR notation in under 30 seconds per question.
Know Your Port Numbers
FTP 20/21, SSH 22, Telnet 23, SMTP 25, DNS 53, HTTP 80, HTTPS 443, RDP 3389, SNMP 161. These appear as answer choices constantly.
OSI Model in Order

Exam Tips
Security Operations is 28%
The largest domain. Focus heavily on incident response procedures, log analysis, SIEM tools, and endpoint protection. TryHackMe SOC labs are perfect prep for this domain.
Know Your Attack Types
Phishing vs spear phishing vs whaling vs vishing vs smishing. SQL injection, XSS, CSRF, buffer overflow, DDoS. These appear on almost every question set.
Cryptography Matters
Understand symmetric vs asymmetric encryption, TLS, PKI, digital certificates, hashing (MD5, SHA-256), and key exchange. Know which algorithms are deprecated.
Application of Concepts
The SY0-701 exam heavily tests application of concepts, not just memorization.

Build a Linux Home Lab

The XK0-005 version added more scripting and automation content. Write at least 10 real bash scripts before exam day — loops, conditionals, cron jobs, and log parsing.

Exam Tips
IP Connectivity = 25% of Exam
The biggest domain. Master OSPF configuration, the show ip route output, and how routing decisions are made. Configure OSPF from scratch in Packet Tracer at least 20 times.
Simulation Questions (Sims)
CCNA includes interactive Cisco IOS simulations. You type real commands and the simulator evaluates your config. Practice in Packet Tracer — there is no substitute for this.
Automation Is Now 10%
The 200-301 version includes network automation basics: REST APIs, JSON, YANG/NETCONF, DNA Center, and Ansible. Know the difference between traditional and controller-based networking.

Complete all 3 free Cisco NetAcad CCNA courses before booking the exam. Each earns a LinkedIn badge. Do this before spending money on the exam voucher.
